Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1) Scope

This policy explains how FreeCVAudit collects, uses, stores, and shares personal data when you use our website, upload CVs, create an account, purchase services, and receive generated outputs.

2) Data We Collect

  • Content data: uploaded CV files, parsed text, generated audits, rewritten CV content, and related metadata.
  • Account/contact data: email address, session identifiers, verification-code metadata, and workspace/account preferences.
  • Payment/transaction data: order IDs, payment provider references, subscription/entitlement status, and fulfillment records (we do not store full card details).
  • Operational/security data: API logs, rate-limit counters, queue and delivery status, and anti-fraud/security events.
  • Analytics data: feature usage events and aggregated service diagnostics.

3) How We Use Data

  • Provide CV audits, rewrites, fulfillment links, and email delivery.
  • Authenticate users, prevent abuse, and protect service integrity.
  • Process purchases, subscriptions, refunds, and billing operations.
  • Monitor performance, troubleshoot incidents, and improve product quality.

4) Legal Bases for Processing

  • Contract: to provide services you request.
  • Legitimate interests: security, fraud prevention, reliability, and service improvement.
  • Consent: where required for optional analytics/communications.
  • Legal obligations: where billing, tax, or regulatory records are required.

5) AI Processing Disclosure

Uploaded CV content is processed by AI services to generate feedback and revised drafts. AI outputs may be inaccurate, incomplete, or biased and should be reviewed by you before use. We do not guarantee interview outcomes, employment decisions, or factual correctness of generated content.

6) Processors and Transfers

We may use service providers such as Netlify (hosting/functions), Google AI providers (content generation), Resend (email delivery), PayPal and WhishPay (payments/subscriptions), and storage/logging services used for operations. Data may be processed in jurisdictions outside your country.

7) Retention

Operational records are retained only as long as needed for fulfillment, security, reconciliation, legal obligations, and reliability controls. Scheduled pruning jobs remove expired records according to configured retention windows. Backup or legal-hold copies may persist for limited periods where required.

8) Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, export, or restriction of your personal data, and may object to certain processing. To submit a request, contact us via the official support channel published on FreeCVAudit.com.

9) Security

We use technical and organizational measures such as access controls, signed sessions/tokens, webhook verification, and rate-limiting controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; please avoid uploading unnecessary sensitive data.

10) Contact

Questions, complaints, and data-rights requests should be sent through the official support/contact channel listed on FreeCVAudit.com.
